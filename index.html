Responsive Design

I am a firm believer in responsive design and giving users a similar experience across all devices. A website should provide the same content across all device sizes and not subtract from the experience based on screen size. Responsive design is the best way to provide website solutions for the wide variety of screen sizes availble while maintaining only one codebase.
